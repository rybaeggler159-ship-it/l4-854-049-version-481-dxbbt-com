2024 美国 电影 犯罪, 剧情 新剧速递

剧情简介

serving a 25-year sentence, Vincent “The King” Morello returns to a New York City he barely recognizes. His once-feared crime family has been dismantled, his territories seized by a coalition of Russian mobsters and a corrupt real estate mogul. Refusing a quiet retirement, Vincent assembles a small crew of loyal old-timers and disillusioned young outsiders. He doesn’t just want his empire back; he wants to reshape the rules of the game. Using a blend of old-school brutality and surprising strategic cunning, he plays the rival factions against each other. The story follows his meticulous, bloody ascent as he confronts not only his enemies but also the ghosts of his past, ultimately questioning what “king” even means in a city that has left him behind.

内容看点

这是一部被暴力美学包裹的古典悲剧。主角的复仇之路清晰而有力，导演对纽约夜景的捕捉阴暗迷人。影片节奏紧凑，每一场权力交涉都充满张力，最后的反转不是关于胜利，而是关于孤独，令人回味无穷。
